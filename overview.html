REALTYMOGUL HAS A DISTINCT FINANCING ADVANTAGE



We offer both equity and preferred equity to real estate investors and companies.


Discretionary capital gives us control over the transaction from start to finish, providing an efficient and transparent process.

FLEXIBLE TERMS


As a source of capital for commercial real estate, we fund projects nationwide with Joint Venture Equity and Preferred Equity. Our agile structure means we are able to close in as few as 30 days to keep projects moving forward.



ELEVATED SERVICE


With a community of investors ready to put their money to work, RealtyMogul serves as the capital markets arm to qualified real estate companies.



WE OFFER A SUITE OF FLEXIBLE FINANCING OPTIONS


RealtyMogul offers Co-GP equity, LP equity and preferred equity to provide custom tailored solutions specific to the deal and business plan. Whether it's common equity or general partner equity, our business plan is to give accretive terms with the versatility to adjust to possible changes in market conditions.

MOGULREIT I FINANCING


MogulREIT I’s objectives are to pay attractive and consistent cash distributions and to preserve, protect, increase, and return stockholders’ capital contributions. As such, MogulREIT I offers Preferred Equity, Joint Venture Equity and Co-General Partner equity with a focus on stabilized transactions that can meet our cash on cash targets.

PRIVATE PLACEMENT FINANCING


The RealtyMogul Private Placement platform offers real estate companies access to small balance capital for value-add transactions across the country. With either Joint Venture Equity or Co-General Partner Equity, RealtyMogul looks to partner with commercial real estate professionals on their value-add transactions.
